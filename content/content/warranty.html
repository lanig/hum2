Warranty

The Hurom Slow Juicer motor is covered by a 10 year warranty and the remaining parts are covered by a two year warranty.

With Hurom products, you are automatically pre-registered for warranty so there is no need for you to complete a warranty registration card. You will, however, need to retain your purchase receipt to prove the date of purchase.

Beware of overseas Hurom products that

  • DO NOT have the correct Australian AC plug
  • HAVE NOT passed Australian Electrical approvals
  • ARE NOT made for Australia's electrical voltage
  • ARE NOT covered under warranty by Hurom Australia
